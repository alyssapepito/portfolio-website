About Me

Hello, my name is Alyssa Pepito and I am an aspiring data analyst with a deep passion for for uncovering insights through data. Though I’m at the beginning of my journey in the world of data analytics, my enthusiasm and commitment to learning are what drive me forward.

I completed my Bachelors in Computer Science (Specialization in Data Science) in Mapúa University, where I developed a strong foundation in data analysis, statistical methods, and data visualization. I’ve gained hands-on experience with tools like SQL, Python, Excel, Tableau, and Power BI, and I’m constantly expanding my skill set to include the latest technologies and methodologies in the field.

I’m eager to start my career in data analysis and am actively seeking opportunities where I can contribute, learn, and further develop my expertise. If you’re looking for a motivated and curious individual to join your team, I’d love to connect!

Data Manipulation with SQL

SQL allows me to efficiently query and manipulate large datasets. I use it to extract, filter, and aggregate data, providing a solid foundation for deeper analysis and insights.

Programming with Python

Python is my go-to language for data analysis and automation. With it, I can clean and analyze data, and create scripts that streamline repetitive tasks, boosting productivity.

Data Visualization with Tableau and Power BI

I use Tableau and Power BI to create dynamic visualizations and dashboards that transform complex data into clear, actionable insights. These tools help me communicate data-driven stories to clients effectively.

Data Management with Excel

Excel is a versatile tool I use for data organization, analysis, and reporting. Whether it’s handling large datasets, performing statistical analysis, or creating pivot tables, Excel is an essential part of my data toolkit.
